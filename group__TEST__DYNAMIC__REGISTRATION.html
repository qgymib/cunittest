Detailed Description

Of course, you can also register your test dynamically without using any of TEST_F(), TEST_P() or TEST().

To dynamically register test, you need to register your test case by cutest_register_case() before cutest_run_tests(), and unregister by cutest_unregister_case() after all test cases are run.

Warning
You cannot unregister test case during cutest_run_tests().
